DESCRIPTION
  • Pure aluminium is a silvery-white metal with many desirable characteristics.
  • It is light, nontoxic (as the metal), nonmagnetic and nonsparking.
  • It is somewhat decorative. It is easily formed, machined, and cast.
  • Pure aluminium is soft and lacks strength, but alloys with small amounts of
    copper, magnesium, silicon, manganese, and other elements have very useful properties.
  • Aluminium is an abundant element in the earth's crust, but it is not found free in nature.
  • The Bayer process is used to refine aluminium from bauxite, an aluminium ore.
