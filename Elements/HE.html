NAME Helium
SYMBOL He
ATOMIC NUMBER 2
APPEARANCE Colourless
CLASSIFICATION Noble gas
BOILING POINT 4.222 K ​
MELTING POINT 0.95 K ​
DESCRIPTION
  • Helium is noble gases. Helium gas is an unreactive and odourless monoatomic gas.
  • Helium is available in pressurised tanks.
  • Helium is the second most abundant element in the universe after hydrogen.
  • α-particles are doubly ionised helium atoms, He2+.
  • Speaking after breathing an atmosphere rich in helium results in a squeaky voice.
  • Helium is present in the atmosphere at about 0.0005% by volume.
  • Its origin in these gases is traced to the decay of radioactive elements in rocks.
