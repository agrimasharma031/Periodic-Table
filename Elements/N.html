NAME Nitrogen
SYMBOL N
ATOMIC NUMBER 7
APPEARANCE colorless gas, liquid or solid
CLASSIFICATION Reactive nonmetal
BOILING POINT 77.355 K ​
MELTING POINT 63.15 K ​
DESCRIPTION
  • Nitrogen is a Group 15 element.
  • Nitrogen makes up about 78% of the atmosphere by volume.
  • The element seemed so inert that Lavoisier named it azote, meaning "without life".
  • However, its compounds are vital components of foods, fertilizers, and explosives.
  • Nitrogen gas is colourless, odourless, and generally inert.
  • As a liquid it is also colourless and odourless.
  • When nitrogen is heated, it combines directly with magnesium, lithium, or calcium.
  • Nitrogen is "fixed" from the atmosphere by bacteria in the roots of
    certain plants such as clover.
