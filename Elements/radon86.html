NAME Radon
SYMBOL Rn
ATOMIC NUMBER 86
APPEARANCE colorless gas
CLASSIFICATION Nobel Gas
BOILING POINT 211.5 K
MELTING POINT 202 K
DESCRIPTION
  • It is a radioactive, colorless, odorless, tasteless, Noble Gas.
  • Radon itself is the immediate decay product of radium.
  • Radon gas is considered as a health hazard.
