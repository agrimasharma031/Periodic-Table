DESCRIPTION
  • Pure germanium is a semiconductor with an appearance similar to elemental silicon.
  • Germanium naturally reacts and forms complexes with oxygen in nature.
  • Germanium ranks near fiftieth in relative abundance of the elements in the Earth's crust.
  • Elemental germanium is used as a semiconductor in transistors and various other electronic devices.
  • Germanium is not thought to be an essential element for any living organism.
  • Germanium is considered a technology-critical element.
