DESCRIPTION
  • Potassium is a metal and is the seventh most abundant and
    makes up about 1.5 % by weight of the earth's crust.
  • Potassium is an essential constituent for plant growth and it is found in most soils.
  • It is also a vital element in the human diet.
  • Potassium is never found free in nature, but is obtained by electrolysis
    of the chloride or hydroxide.
  • It is soft and easily cut with a knife.
  • It is silvery in appearance immediately after a fresh surface is exposed.
