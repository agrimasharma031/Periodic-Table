NAME Lanthanum
SYMBOL La
ATOMIC NUMBER 57
APPEARANCE Silvery white
CLASSIFICATION Lanthanide
BOILING POINT 3737 K
MELTING POINT 1193 K
DESCRIPTION
  • Lanthanum is the first element and prototype of the lanthanide series.
  • It appears to the right of the alkaline earth metal barium.
  • It is to the left of the lanthanide cerium.
  • Lanthanum compounds have numerous applications as catalysts.
