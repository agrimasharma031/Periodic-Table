NAME Magnesium
SYMBOL Mg
ATOMIC NUMBER 12
APPEARANCE shiny grey solid
CLASSIFICATION Alkaline earth metal
BOILING POINT 1363 K ​
MELTING POINT 923 K ​
DESCRIPTION
  • Magnesium is a grayish-white, fairly tough metal.
  • Magnesium is the eighth most abundant element in the
    earth's crust although not found in it's elemental form.
  • It is a Group 2 element, Group 2 elements are called alkaline earth metals.
  • Magnesium metal burns with a very bright light.
  • Magnesium is an important element for plant and animal life.
  • Normally magnesium is coated with a layer of oxide,
    MgO, that protects magnesium from air and water.
