NAME Meitnerium
SYMBOL Mt
ATOMIC NUMBER 109
APPEARANCE Unknown, but probably metallic and silvery white or grey
CLASSIFICATION Metallic
BOILING POINT Unknown
MELTING POINT Unknown
DESCRIPTION
  • Meitnerium is a synthetic chemical element with the symbol Mt and atomic number 109.
  • It is an extremely radioactive synthetic element.
  • The most stable known isotope, meitnerium-278, has a half-life of 4.5 seconds, although the unconfirmed
    meitnerium-282 may have a longer half-life of 67 seconds.
