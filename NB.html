NAME Niobium
SYMBOL Nb
ATOMIC NUMBER 41
APPEARANCE Light grey crystal
CLASSIFICATION Transition Metal
BOILING POINT 5014 K
MELTING POINT 2750 K
DESCRIPTION
  • Niobium is used in alloys including stainless steel.
  • It improves the strength of the alloys, particularly at low temperatures.
  • This element also has superconducting properties.
  • It is very resistant to corrosion due to a layer of oxide on its surface.
