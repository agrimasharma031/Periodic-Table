NAME Sodium

BOILING POINT 1156.090 K ​

DESCRIPTION
  • Sodium is a Group 1 element (or IA in older labelling styles).
  • Group 1 elements are often referred to as the "alkali metals".
  • The chemistry of sodium is dominated by the +1 ion Na+.
  • Soap is generally a sodium salt of fatty acids.
  • The importance of common salt to animal nutrition has been
    recognized since prehistoric times.
  • The most common compound is sodium chloride, (table salt).
