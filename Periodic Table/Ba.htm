NAME Barium
SYMBOL Ba
ATOMIC NUMBER 56
APPEARANCE Silvery gray; with a pale yellow tint
CLASSIFICATION Alkaline Earth Metal
BOILING POINT 2118 K
MELTING POINT 1000 K
DESCRIPTION
  • Barium is a soft, silvery-white metal, with a slight golden shade.
  • The silvery-white color of barium metal rapidly vanishes upon oxidation in air yielding a dark gray oxide layer.
  • Barium has a medium specific weight.
  • It has good electrical conductivity.
