NAME Caesium
SYMBOL Cs
ATOMIC NUMBER 55
APPEARANCE Pale Yellow
CLASSIFICATION Alkali Metal
BOILING POINT 944 K
MELTING POINT 301.7 K
DESCRIPTION
  • The most reactive of all metals.
  • It is pyrophoric and reacts with water even at −116 °C (−177 °F).
  • It is the least electronegative element, with a value of 0.79 on the Pauling scale.
  • It has only one stable isotope, caesium[133].
