NAME Rhodium
SYMBOL Rh
ATOMIC NUMBER 45
APPEARANCE Silvery white metallic
CLASSIFICATION Transition Metal
BOILING POINT 3968 K
MELTING POINT 2237 K
DESCRIPTION
  • Rhodium is found in platinum or nickel ores together with the other members of the platinum group metals.
  • Rhodium metal is inert against corrosion and most aggressive chemicals.
  • Rhodium detectors are used in nuclear reactors to measure the neutron flux level.
  • Other uses of rhodium include asymmetric hydrogenation used to form drug precursors.
