NAME Tellurium
SYMBOL Te
ATOMIC NUMBER 52
APPEARANCE Silvery lustrous gray
CLASSIFICATION Metalloid
BOILING POINT 1261 K
MELTING POINT 722.66 K
DESCRIPTION
  • Tellurium has two allotropes, crystalline and amorphous.
  • When crystalline, tellurium is silvery-white with a metallic luster.
  • It is a brittle and easily pulverized metalloid.
