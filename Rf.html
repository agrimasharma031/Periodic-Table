NAME Ruthorfordium
SYMBOL Rf
ATOMIC NUMBER 104
APPEARANCE unknown, but probably metallic and silvery white or grey
CLASSIFICATION Metallic
BOILING POINT 5800 K
MELTING POINT 2400 K
DESCRIPTION
  • As a synthetic element, it is not found in nature and can only be created in a laboratory.
  • It is radioactive; the most stable known isotope, Rf, has a half-life of approximately 1.3 hours.
  • It does not have any stable or naturally occurring isotopes.
