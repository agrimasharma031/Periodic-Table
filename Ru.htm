NAME Ruthenium
SYMBOL Ru
ATOMIC NUMBER 44
APPEARANCE Silvery white metallic
CLASSIFICATION Transition Metal
BOILING POINT 4423
MELTING POINT 2607 K
DESCRIPTION
  • Ruthenium has four crystal modifications and does not tarnish at ambient conditions.
  • It oxidizes upon heating to 800 °C (1,070 K).
  • Ruthenium dissolves in fused alkalis to give ruthenates (RuO2− 4).
  • is not attacked by acids
