NAME Xenon
SYMBOL Xe
ATOMIC NUMBER 54
APPEARANCE colorless gas, exhibiting a blue glow when placed in an electric field
CLASSIFICATION Noble gas
BOILING POINT 165.051 K
MELTING POINT 161.40 K
DESCRIPTION
  • Xenon is used in flash lamps and arc lamps, and as a general anesthetic.
  • It consists of seven stable isotopes.
  • It has two long-lived radioactive isotopes.
