NAME Actinium
SYMBOL Ac
ATOMIC NUMBER 89
APPEARANCE silver-white glowing with an eerie blue light
CLASSIFICATION Actinide, sometimes considered a transition metal
BOILING POINT 3500+-300 K
MELTING POINT 1500 K
DESCRIPTION
  • A soft, silvery-white radioactive metal, actinium reacts rapidly with oxygen and
    moisture in air forming a white coating of actinium oxide that prevents further oxidation.
  • It is also sometimes considered the first of the 7th-period transition metals.
  • Actinium gave the name to the actinide series, a group of 15 similar elements between
    actinium and lawrencium in the periodic table.
