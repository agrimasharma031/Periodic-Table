NAME Dubnium
SYMBOL Db
ATOMIC NUMBER 105
APPEARANCE Unknown, but probably metallic and silvery white or grey
CLASSIFICATION Metallic
BOILING POINT Unknown
MELTING POINT Unknown
DESCRIPTION
  • Dubnium is highly radioactive.
  • he most stable known isotope, dubnium-268, has a half-life of about 28 hours.
  • This greatly limits the extent of research on dubnium.
