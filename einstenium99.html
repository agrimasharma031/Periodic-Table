NAME einstenium
SYMBOL Es
ATOMIC NUMBER 99
APPEARANCE silvery; glows blue in the dark
CLASSIFICATION Actinide
BOILING POINT 1269 K
MELTING POINT 1133 K
DESCRIPTION
  • Einsteinium is a soft, silvery, paramagnetic metal.
  • The high radioactivity of einsteinium-253 produces a visible glow and rapidly damages its crystalline metal lattice, with released heat of about 1000 watts per gram.
  • Einsteinium is the element with the highest atomic number which has been observed in macroscopic quantities in its pure form, and this was the common short-lived isotope einsteinium-253.[2]
