NAME Protactinium
SYMBOL Pa
ATOMIC NUMBER 91
APPEARANCE bright, silvery metallic luster
CLASSIFICATION actinide
BOILING POINT 4300 K
MELTING POINT 1841 K
DESCRIPTION
  • It is a dense, silvery-gray actinide metal which readily reacts with oxygen, water vapor and inorganic acid.
  • protactinium is mostly extracted from spent nuclear fuel.
  • It forms various chemical compounds in which protactinium is usually present in the oxidation state +5, but it can also assume +4 and even +3 or +2 states.
