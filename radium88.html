NAME Radium
SYMBOL Ra
ATOMIC NUMBER 88
APPEARANCE silver-white-metallic
CLASSIFICATION Alkaline earth Metal
BOILING POINT 2010 K
MELTING POINT 973 K
DESCRIPTION
  • It is 6th element in group 2 of periodic element.
  • Radium is silvery-white, but it readily reacts with nitrogen on exposure to air, forming a black surface layer of radium nitride (Ra3N2).
  • Currently, other than its use in nuclear medicine, radium has no commercial applications.
