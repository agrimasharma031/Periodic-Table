NAME Thorium
SYMBOL Th
ATOMIC NUMBER 90
APPEARANCE silvery, often with black tarnish
CLASSIFICATION actinide
BOILING POINT 5061 K
MELTING POINT 2023 K
DESCRIPTION
  • Thorium is an electropositive actinide whose chemistry is dominated by the +4 oxidation state.
  • Thorium is silvery and tarnishes black when it is exposed to air, forming thorium dioxide.
  • On Earth, hydrogen is found in the greatest quantities as water.
  • it is moderately hard, malleable, and has a high melting point .
